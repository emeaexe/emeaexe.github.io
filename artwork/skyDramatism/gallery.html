Sky Dramatism

(2021)

The Sky Dramatism series is a short practice photographic series. Featuring pictures of dramatic skies, it remembers us to look at the skies more often, sometimes, just to get an awesome view.

Technique: Paper negatives on Kentmere VC Select.

Developed in Rude Rodinal C (homemade rodinal-like concoction)

Asahi Pentax K1000, SMC Pentax 1:2 50mm

Digital inversions of physical negatives.
